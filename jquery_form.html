Packages

All Inclusive

All inclusive holidays are most popular with families.
When you book this package most if not all services are included within the price.

Included within the package:
Accomodation
three daily meals
Soft Drinks
Most Alholic Drinks
Some may even offer extras

Full Board

Full Board is popular cost effective Holiday
You do not need to purchase food when you are booked on a Full Board Holiday


Included in the package:
Accommodaiton
Breakfast Every morning
Evening Meal each night
All you have to purchase is any drink

Half Board

Cost effective Holidays for families
You need to pay for your dinner on a Half Board Holiday

included in package:

Breakfast every morning
Evening Meal each evening
Accommodation

Self Catering

Self Catering rooms usually have a kitchen within them.
Guests have to cater for themselves for all meals.

Rooms usually include pots and pans to enable guests to cater for themselves.
This allows for more flexibility to allow people to eat out.
